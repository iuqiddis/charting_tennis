The Dataset

The crowdsourced CC-licensed Match Charting Project dataset consists of:

  • 6,000+ matches from the men's ATP and women's WTA tennis tours.
  • 300,000+ points
  • 2 million+ shots with detailed information, such as the type of stroke, direction of shot, depth, rally length, etc.

Who would benefit from my analysis:

  • Tennis professionals (a niche market)
  • Prediction sites (Gaming, Fantasy Tennis)
  • Amateur tennis players with sensor data from smart devices
